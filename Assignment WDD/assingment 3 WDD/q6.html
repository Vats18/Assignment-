1. Tawang, Arunachal Pradesh


Tawang is blessed with breathtaking views and a vibrant mix of Indian and Chinese culture. March is the best time to enjoy the trekking routes, the famous Tibetan festival of Losar and the orchid sanctuary, which will be in full bloom at this time. Peace and favourable weather make Tawang one of the best places to go in March in India.

2. Ranthambore, Rajasthan


The jewel of Sawai Madhopur, Ranthambore has one of the best tiger reserves in the country. During March, the chances of spotting tigers increase as it gets warm enough for them to come out of the forest in search of water. Travellers can also spot a variety of colourful birds while in Ranthambore.

3. Rishikesh, Uttarakhand


The gateway to the Garhwal Himalayas, Rishikesh is the best place to visit in March in India as tourists from all over the world visit this place to attend the International Yoga Festival. For adventure lovers, the city boasts of India’s highest bungee jumping spot, river rafting, ziplining and many more thrilling activities. Travellers must attend Ganga Aarti at any of Rishikesh’s many ghats.
